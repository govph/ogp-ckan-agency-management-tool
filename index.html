Open Government Data Manager/Agency Management Tool

The platform is geared towards making data submission, review, clean-up, and publishing a more stream-lined process on the Open Data platforms. Making it easier for the system users (Data Administration Team and Gov't Agencies) to upload and manage data that goes into the system.
